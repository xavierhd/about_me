About me

Ingenious thinker, agile communicator, engineer, programmer and manager, I dream of improving people's quality of life, the productivity of society, by avoiding waste, optimizing travel and the use of available resources, and circularizing products and services.

I want to work with people who share my passion for freedom of choice, of offering more options instead of dumbing down services, who promote playful education and ease of use, where a clear why and a simple how for the user are paramount. I am committed to respecting privacy, the customer remains a customer and not a product.

On the technical side, I value thourough research before headfirst implementation, refactor over quick fixes, and most of all, creative use of design patterns, data structures, evolving prototypes and testing over fireman code.
